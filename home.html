About

BIRA Football Academy is a football academy out to act as a link between talented players and their desired goal. We prepare them all round to be ready for a professional life. It is a fast rising academy and have accounted for the sale of rising talents to european clubs.

AIM

Over the years nigeria has produced lots of great talent who never made it to the world stage. Many who placed all hopes in this risky outcome game of football, all now left shattered as a result of not achieving their goals. We are out to help bridge the gap and help as many as we can.

PLAYERS TREATMENT

We have one of the best player treatment policy, we allow them taste a feel of professional treatment even before they go pro. we've got a large camp, well served nutrition based three square meal, good sleep time, and great tours program across the world.
